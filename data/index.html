    Guidelines
    All
    • Creatinine Clearance (Cockcroft-Gault Equation)
      Calculates CrCl according to the Cockcroft-Gault equation.
    • CKD-EPI Equations for Glomerular Filtration Rate (GFR)
      Estimates GFR based on serum creatinine, serum cystatin C, or both.
    • CHA2DS2-VASc Score for Atrial Fibrillation Stroke Risk
      Calculates stroke risk for patients with atrial fibrillation, possibly better than the CHADS₂ Score.
    • Calcium Correction for Hypoalbuminemia
      Calculates a corrected calcium level for patients with hypoalbuminemia.
    • Mean Arterial Pressure (MAP)
      Calculates mean arterial pressure.
    • ASCVD (Atherosclerotic Cardiovascular Disease) 2013 Risk Calculator from AHA/ACC
      Determines 10-year risk of heart disease or stroke.
    • Body Mass Index (BMI) and Body Surface Area (BSA)
      Calculates body mass index and body surface area.
    • MDRD GFR Equation
      Estimates glomerular filtration rate based on creatinine and patient characteristics.
    • Pregnancy Due Dates Calculator
      Calculates pregnancy dates from last period, gestational age, or date of conception.
    • Wells' Criteria for Pulmonary Embolism
      Objectifies risk of pulmonary embolism.
    • Corrected QT Interval (QTc)
      Corrects the QT interval for heart rate extremes (choose from Bazett, Fridericia, Framingham, Hodges, or Rautaharju formulas).
    • Ideal Body Weight and Adjusted Body Weight
      Calculates ideal body weight (Devine formula) and adjusted body weight.
    • Revised Cardiac Risk Index for Pre-Operative Risk
      Estimates risk of cardiac complications after noncardiac surgery.
    • Child-Pugh Score for Cirrhosis Mortality
      Estimates cirrhosis severity.
    • NIH Stroke Scale/Score (NIHSS)
      Calculates the NIH Stroke Scale for quantifying stroke severity.
    • HEART Score for Major Cardiac Events
      Predicts 6-week risk of major adverse cardiac event.
    • MELD Na (UNOS/OPTN)
      Quantifies end-stage liver disease for transplant planning with sodium.
    • Sodium Correction for Hyperglycemia
      Calculates the actual sodium level in patients with hyperglycemia.
    • Steroid Conversion Calculator
      Converts steroid dosages using dosing equivalencies.
    • PHQ-9 (Patient Health Questionnaire-9)
      Objectifies degree of depression severity.
    • Wells' Criteria for DVT
      Calculates risk of DVT based on clinical criteria.
    • Maintenance Fluids Calculations
      Calculates maintenance fluid requirements by weight.
    • HAS-BLED Score for Major Bleeding Risk
      Estimates risk of major bleeding for patients on anticoagulation to assess risk-benefit in atrial fibrillation care.
    • Morphine Milligram Equivalents (MME) Calculator
      Calculates total daily morphine milligram equivalents.
    • Centor Score (Modified/McIsaac) for Strep Pharyngitis
      Estimates probability that pharyngitis is streptococcal, and suggests management course.
    • Glasgow Coma Scale/Score (GCS)
      Coma severity based on Eye (4), Verbal (5), and Motor (6) criteria.
    • Fractional Excretion of Sodium (FENa)
      Determines if renal failure is due to pre-renal, intrinsic, or post-renal pathology.
    • PERC Rule for Pulmonary Embolism
      Rules out PE if no criteria are present and pre-test probability is ≤15%.
    • STOP-BANG Score for Obstructive Sleep Apnea
      Screens for obstructive sleep apnea.
    • Caprini Score for Venous Thromboembolism (2005)
      Stratifies risk of VTE in surgical patients.
    • Sequential Organ Failure Assessment (SOFA) Score
      Predicts ICU mortality based on lab results and clinical data.
    • GAD-7 (General Anxiety Disorder-7)
      Measures severity of anxiety.
    • Framingham Risk Score for Hard Coronary Heart Disease
      Estimates 10-year risk of heart attack.
    • Fibrosis-4 (FIB-4) Index for Liver Fibrosis
      Noninvasive estimate of liver scarring in HCV and HBV patients, to assess need for biopsy.
    • SIRS, Sepsis, and Septic Shock Criteria
      Defines the severity of sepsis and septic shock.
    • Anion Gap
      Evaluates states of metabolic acidosis.
    • MELDNa/MELD-Na Score for Liver Cirrhosis
      Adds sodium to the MELD model for liver cirrhosis.
    • Free Water Deficit in Hypernatremia
      Calculates free water deficit by estimated total body water.
    • ASCVD (Atherosclerotic Cardiovascular Disease) Risk Algorithm including Known ASCVD from AHA/ACC
      Determines 10-year risk of heart disease or stroke and provides statin recommendations.
    • Arterial Blood Gas (ABG) Analyzer
      Interprets ABG.
    • Gupta Perioperative Risk for Myocardial Infarction or Cardiac Arrest (MICA)
      Predicts risk of MI or cardiac arrest after surgery.
    • Serum Osmolality/Osmolarity
      Calculates expected serum osmolarity, for comparison to measured osmolarity to detect unmeasured compounds in the serum.
    • CIWA-Ar for Alcohol Withdrawal
      The CIWA-Ar objectifies severity of alcohol withdrawal.
    • CURB-65 Score for Pneumonia Severity
      Estimates mortality of community-acquired pneumonia to help determine inpatient vs. outpatient treatment.
    • PECARN Pediatric Head Injury/Trauma Algorithm
      Predicts need for brain imaging after pediatric head injury.
    • ARISCAT Score for Postoperative Pulmonary Complications
      Predicts risk of pulmonary complications after surgery, including respiratory failure.
    • LDL Calculated
      Calculates LDL based on total and HDL cholesterol and triglycerides.
    • APACHE II Score
      Estimates ICU mortality
    • ABCD2 Score for TIA
      Estimates the risk of stroke after a suspected transient ischemic attack (TIA).
    • Maddrey's Discriminant Function for Alcoholic Hepatitis
      Suggests which patients with alcoholic hepatitis may have a poor prognosis and benefit from steroid administration.
